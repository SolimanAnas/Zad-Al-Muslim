Introduction

Zad Al-Muslim is completely free, ad-free, requires no account, and never sells or shares your data with any marketing party. The app is designed to run mostly on your device, offline.

Data stored only on your device

Settings and progress are saved locally (theme, language, bookmarks, last-read page, dhikr/tasbih counters, and statistics). To compute prayer times and Qibla direction, an approximate location (rounded to about 1 km) is stored on your device. Clearing the app's data or using "Factory Reset" permanently erases all of it.

Prayer notifications (optional)

If you enable prayer-time notifications, the app sends only the following to our notification server (Cloudflare Workers): your browser's push subscription and a 7-day prayer-time schedule computed from your approximate location. This is used exclusively to deliver notifications on time and is deleted automatically when you unsubscribe or the subscription expires. Your precise location is never stored on the server.

Third-party services

Some features connect to external services governed by their own policies: Quran text, tafsir and recitations (api.quran.com, alquran.cloud, mp3quran.net and similar), live Quran radio streams, YouTube videos on the video page (Google/YouTube privacy policy applies), and Google Fonts on some pages.
